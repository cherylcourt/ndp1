About Me

I've always had a passion for solving problems and creating things for as long as I can remember. Software development was a natural fit for me and I've been successfully crafting software solutions for more than ten years now. Beyond continuously building my technical skills I also like to delve into the human factor involved in software development ranging from client interaction to team dynamics. I believe clear communication and mutual respect are key in determining project success.

When I'm not busy developing software you might find me playing with my daughter or working on a scene for one of my acting classes among a number of other things (because life is short, y'know; although there always seems to be time for video games and tv).
